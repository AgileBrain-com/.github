# AgileBrain

Building the future of Motivation Intelligence. We make human potential measurable and actionable through AI systems, agents, and developer tools.

## What we build

AgileBrain helps organizations understand and act on what drives people. Our image exercises and motivation models turn something that used to be soft and slow into something measurable and fast.

## Building in the open

We open source the building blocks and the integration surface that make AgileBrain easy to build with. We keep our core models and platform private. That boundary is deliberate. It lets partners move fast while our proprietary methods stay protected.

Two efforts are rolling out shortly:

- An Embed Kit, the shortest path for partner teams from interested to running, with copy-paste examples and a documented integration surface.
- A Community Components library, a governed home for generic, reusable modules, with a focus on giving student builders real, public, portfolio-grade work.

## For student and community builders

We believe in giving the next generation of engineers genuine public work to point to. As our community library opens, every accepted contribution is credited to its author.
